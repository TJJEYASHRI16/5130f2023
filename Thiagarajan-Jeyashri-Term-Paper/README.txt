#This is a file containing information about Term Paper on Chatbots used in Websites 

Abstract:
In the digital realm, chatbots play a vital role on websites, offering a conversational interface to assist and engage users. This paper explores the significance of chatbots, their various types, practical applications, and the associated challenges. Chatbots, designed for user-friendly interactions, serve as efficient solutions for web-based inquiries and operational streamlining. However, they also confront issues such as continuous learning requirements and data privacy concerns. Understanding the multifaceted nature of chatbot technology is essential for optimizing their contributions to websites and user experiences.
